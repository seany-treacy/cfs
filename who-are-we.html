Who Are We

We are one of the fastest growing independent wholesalers!

Core Facilities Solutions was founded in 2024 as a true “one-stop solution” wholesaler dedicated to exclusively supporting the facilities maintenance industry. With over 50 years of combined wholesale experience, our leadership team brings deep industry knowledge and proven expertise to CFS. This strong foundation has helped us become one of the fastest-growing independent wholesalers in the UK, proudly supporting and supplying some of the most prestigious buildings across the country.

 

As a family-orientated business, we pride ourselves on values built around trust, reliability, and long-term partnerships. We treat every customer, supplier, and partner with the same respect and commitment that define strong families — creating a service experience that is personal, supportive, and consistently dependable.
